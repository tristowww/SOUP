## Experimental Phase and Implications {#slide-18}

![A robot with a face](ppt/media/image49.jpeg "Picture 26")

30 employees who were recently hired

Experimental phase 1 - 15 of these employees (50%, group 1) only utilized the chatbot prototype while others (group 2) utilized went the more traditional route

Experimental phase 2 -- group 2 interacted with the chatbot prototype

Focused on usability, recommendation, presentation, dialogue and  future development

Dialogue needs improvement, great on everything else, recommendation was especially reassuring 

Final evaluation for both groups via a Likert scale

Simplified learning

For future, better experimental phase and further utilizing the chatbot
