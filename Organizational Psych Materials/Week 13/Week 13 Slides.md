## Microaggressions  {#slide-15}

-   Microaggressions:  Brief and commonplace daily verbal, behavioral, or environmental indignities rooted in stereotypes that communicate hostile, derogatory, or negative prejudicial slights and insults towards any underrepresented group
-  Stems from stereotypes and system1 thinking 

<!-- -->

- Does not consider another person's point of view before thinking

<!-- -->

-  The perpetrators are often unaware of their microaggressions 

<!-- -->

- Can be in the form of a verbal or behavioral message

<!-- -->

-  Can have real negative outcomes such as depression, loneliness, anxiety
