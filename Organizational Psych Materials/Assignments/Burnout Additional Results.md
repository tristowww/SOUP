## Hypothesis 2 (Correlational) {#slide-26}

Students with a higher number of roles will report greater levels of role overload and, consequently, higher burnout. 

## Slide 27

![](ppt/media/image22.png "Picture 2")

- Number of Roles - Role Overload: r = 0.090 (ns )
- Number of Roles - Burnout: r = -0.101 (ns)
- Role Overload - Burnout: r = 0.577 (\*\*\*)

## Hypothesis 3 (Mediation) {#slide-28}

Role overload will mediate the relationship between the number of roles and burnout, such that the number of roles will positively predict role overload, which in turn will predict higher burnout.
