Thank you for considering participating in the following survey on the stress experiences of students at Radford University.
The goal of this survey is to understand how to better support Radford students in balancing the demands from various
areas of life.
Please only complete this survey if you are a current student at Radford University.
Confidentiality
Please be assured that your responses will be kept strictly confidential. The data collected will be used for research
purposes and will be anonymized to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of all participants. There is no right or wrong
answers, we are interested in your honest and candid responses.
Estimated Time
The survey should take approximately 30 minutes to complete. We appreciate you taking the time to share your experiences
and insights.
Consent
By proceeding to the next section, you indicate your consent to participate in this survey.
If you are ready to begin the survey, please proceed. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to Dr.
Teresa Ristow at tristow@radford.edu.
