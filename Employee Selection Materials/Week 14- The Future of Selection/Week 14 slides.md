## NYC AI Bias Audit Mandate {#slide-20}

New York City's legislation as a response to growing concerns over AI bias in hiring and employment decisions

- Effective in Jan,1,2023
- Requires employers and employment agencies to conduct bias audits of their automated employment decision tools

https://www.nyc.gov/site/dca/about/automated-employment-decision-tools.page

20

## NYC AI Bias Audit Mandate: Key Components {#slide-21}

/

21

::: {.smartart .list1 layout="list1"}
**Bias audit requirements: **

- Regular follow up audits 
- Independent assessment of the automated tools to eval their impact on fairness across different demographic groups

**Disclosure requirements:**

- Employers must disclose to candidates when automated tools are used in the assessment
- Bias audit must be publicly posted 

**Definition of automated tools:**

- Systems that use ML or AI processes to make or assist in employment decisions
:::
