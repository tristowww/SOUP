## Speer, 2018 {#slide-21}

Key Findings:

-  Narrative sentiment scores: reliable and correlate with traditional numeric ratings
-  Incremental prediction: narrative scores add unique insights in prediction beyond numeric ratings, predicting outcomes like promotions and turnover
-  Narrative scores enhance the robustness of performance evals by being construct valid
