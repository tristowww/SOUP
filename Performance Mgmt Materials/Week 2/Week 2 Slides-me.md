## History of Performance Management {#slide-3}

3

- WW1- Formal appraisal with Army rating/ranking system based on merit
- 1920s- Forced rank used
- 1940s- Behavioral methods used
- 1960s- Self-evaluation
- 1970s- Focus on merit-based pay 
- 1980s- Forced ranking utilized in GE
- 1990s- 360-degree feedback and appraisal developed
- 2000s- Flatter organizations
- 2010s- Several larger orgs. move away for formal annual appraisal for more continuous options

## Evolution of Performance Management {#slide-4}

4

- Early Focus  -- emphasis on performance appraisal systems with rating scales
- Modern PM  -- integrated process focusing on continuous feedback, goal alignment and employee development

- Shift from appraisal to management
