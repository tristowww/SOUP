# Online Courses Directory

This directory contains the working files for ten commercial online courses being developed for the Kajabi platform. Courses 1-7 target employers, HR professionals, and managers. Courses 8-10 form a **job-seeker vertical** targeting candidates, career changers, and career coaches — leveraging Teresa's unique position as someone who designs the hiring systems candidates are trying to navigate. Each course adapts graduate-level I/O Psychology academic materials (from the parent repository) into practitioner-focused, commercially viable online courses.

**Subject Matter Expert:** Dr. Teresa Ristow (Virginia Tech PhD, I/O Psychology)
**Platform:** Kajabi
**Primary Differentiator:** Intersection of traditional I/O psychology with applied AI, NLP, and data science methods

## Proposed Work

### For Each Course Subdirectory

Each course subdirectory will contain the following as development progresses:

1. **`overview.md`** (created) - Course architecture, audience, differentiators, source materials, MVP scope, and pricing from the strategy document
2. **Module scripts** - Practitioner-friendly lesson scripts adapted from academic source materials
3. **Templates and tools** - Downloadable checklists, worksheets, scorecards, and calculators for each module
4. **Assessment questions** - Self-assessments and knowledge checks (adapted from academic exams, not copied)
5. **Case studies** - Real-world practitioner scenarios illustrating key concepts
6. **Slide decks / visual assets** - Presentation materials for video recording

### Cross-Cutting Work

- **Content adaptation:** Convert academic materials to practitioner language following the adaptation rules in the root CLAUDE.md
- **Template creation:** Every module should end with a downloadable tool (scorecard, checklist, worksheet, calculator)
- **Video planning:** Determine format (talking head, slides + voiceover, screen share, hybrid) after pilot recording
- **Kajabi buildout:** Site architecture, sales pages, email sequences, community setup
- **SHRM/HRCI credit pursuit:** Apply for continuing education credit approval for Courses 1-3

### Launch Sequencing

1. **Months 2-3:** Course 7 (gateway/lead magnet) to build email list
2. **Months 3-4:** Courses 1 and 2 (highest content readiness, broadest appeal)
3. **Months 5-7:** Courses 3 and 5 (Course 5 is Teresa's flagship AI course)
4. **Months 6-8:** Courses 4 and 6 (premium/cohort-based offerings)
5. **Months 6-7:** Course 8 (job-seeker vertical launch — candidate-side perspective flip of selection content + dissertation)
6. **Months 7-8:** Course 9 (employer evaluation — candidate-side perspective flip of org psych content + dissertation)
7. **Months 9-10:** Course 10 (train-the-trainer for career coaches — repackaged Courses 8-9 + coaching methodology)

## Content Adaptation Rules

When working in this directory, follow the content adaptation rules defined in the root `CLAUDE.md`:

- Strip academic citation format; move to "Further Reading" sections
- Replace psychometric terminology with plain-language equivalents
- Add actionable templates, checklists, or tools to every module
- Use "evidence shows" not "studies suggest"
- Use "practitioners" not "students"; "your organization" not "the organization"
- Active voice, second person, imperative mood for instructions
- Do not fabricate statistics or research findings
- Do not use copyrighted article text verbatim
