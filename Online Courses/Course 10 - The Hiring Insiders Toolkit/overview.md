## Audience

**Primary:** Career Coaches, Outplacement Professionals, & University Career Centers (Segment 12)
**Secondary:** HR Consultants (Segment 8), I/O Psych Students/Early-Career (Segment 4)

---

## Differentiator

Career coaching is a $2B+ industry built largely on anecdote and personal experience. This course gives coaches and career services professionals the same I/O psychology research base that informs hiring system design — Campion's structured interview framework, validity evidence for selection methods, person-job fit theory, and Teresa's dissertation on deceptive job ads — packaged into client-ready coaching frameworks. It is the only train-the-trainer career coaching program grounded in published selection science and organizational psychology research.

## Format

Self-paced core: 6-8 modules, ~10 hours total content. Each module: 15-min video lesson + client-facing deliverable template + implementation guide. Workshop-enhanced version adds 4 live group coaching practice sessions (bi-weekly). B2B license includes customization consultation and bulk access.

## MVP Scope (4 Core Modules)

1. **The Science Your Clients Don't Know** - Complete overview of how hiring systems actually work (selection methods, validity evidence, decision-making models) — the knowledge base that separates evidence-based coaches from advice-givers
2. **Client Assessment & Career Fit Frameworks** - Person-job fit, person-org fit, satisfaction predictors, and career values assessment tools adapted for coaching practice
3. **Teaching Clients to Navigate Modern Hiring** - Coaching protocols for resume optimization, interview preparation (structured and unstructured), assessment navigation, and AI screening — with client-facing handouts
4. **The Red Flag Toolkit** - Teresa's dissertation research packaged for coaches: how to teach clients to evaluate job ads, assess employer credibility, and make informed career decisions

### Client Implementation Guides (included with each module)
- Resume review protocol with scoring rubric

- Job ad analysis framework (client-facing version of dissertation findings)
