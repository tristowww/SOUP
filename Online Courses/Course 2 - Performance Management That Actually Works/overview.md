# Course 2: Performance Management That Actually Works

## Full Title
Performance Management That Actually Works: From Annual Reviews to Continuous Systems

## Launch Priority
Launch first (Month 3-4)

## Price Tier
Standard ($197-297); Premium with Analytics Add-on ($397)

---

## Audience

**Primary:** HR Professionals (Segment 1), People Managers (Segment 2)
**Secondary:** Small Business Owners (Segment 3), Nonprofit Leaders (Segment 5), Tech People Ops (Segment 7)

---

## Differentiator

Teaches PM as a system, not just a form. Grounds recommendations in 100+ years of research while providing immediately usable tools. Includes bias identification training backed by research on 17+ specific bias types. The only PM course that teaches practitioners to compute and interpret interrater reliability.

---

## Core Topic Clusters

- 2.1: PM Foundations (PM vs. appraisal, PM cycle, evidence-based practices)
- 2.2: Performance Criteria & Measurement (BARS, GRS, RPM, task vs. contextual performance)
- 2.3: Multi-Source Feedback (360-degree feedback, rater training, interrater reliability)
- 2.4: Motivation & Development (expectancy theory, equity theory, goal-setting)
- 2.5: Bias, Fairness, & Ethics (17+ bias types, implicit person theory, justice framework)

---

Self-paced, 8-10 modules, ~12 hours total content. Includes optional "Analytics Add-on" module with Excel/Python exercises.
