---

## Differentiator

Job search courses focus on getting hired. This course focuses on getting hired at the right place. Teresa's I/O psychology expertise in job satisfaction predictors, person-job and person-organization fit, organizational culture assessment, and motivation theory gives candidates a research-backed framework for evaluating employers — not just impressing them. Her dissertation on deceptive job ad language adds a unique "buyer beware" dimension no career coach can replicate.

## MVP Scope (4 Core Modules)

1. **What Actually Predicts Job Satisfaction** - Research on person-job fit, person-org fit, and the factors that predict whether you'll thrive or burn out (spoiler: salary is not the strongest predictor)
2. **Reading Organizational Culture from the Outside** - How to assess culture during the interview process using research-backed signals, the right questions to ask, and what to look for beyond the career page
3. **The Science of Career Decisions** - Self-determination theory, goal-setting research, and expectancy theory applied to career choices: a framework for decisions you won't regret
4. **Due Diligence: Evaluating Offers and Spotting Red Flags** - Teresa's dissertation research on deceptive job ad language, Glassdoor signal vs. noise, and an evidence-based framework for evaluating whether an employer is what they claim to be

### MVP Deliverables
