**[Sample BARS Table for Written Responses]{.mark}**

**[Dimension 1: Comprehensiveness of the Answer]{.mark}**

- [**1/5 (Minimal):** Response lacks key points; only 10-20% of relevant topics covered.]{.mark}

- [**3/5 (Adequate):** Covers most key points; 60-70% of topics addressed with limited depth.]{.mark}

- [**5/5 (Mastery):** Fully addresses all relevant topics; comprehensive coverage with in-depth explanations.]{.mark}

**[Dimension 2: Inclusion of Relevant Subtopics]{.mark}**

- [**1/5 (Minimal):** Few or no subtopics mentioned; major omissions in coverage.]{.mark}

- [**3/5 (Adequate):** Some subtopics included; partial coverage of key areas.]{.mark}

- [**5/5 (Mastery):** All relevant subtopics included; complete and relevant detail provided.]{.mark}

**[Dimension 3: Clarity and Coherence of Arguments]{.mark}**

- [**1/5 (Minimal):** Disorganized response; arguments lack logic and flow.]{.mark}

- [**3/5 (Adequate):** Somewhat organized; arguments make sense but lack fluidity.]{.mark}

- [**5/5 (Mastery):** Clear, coherent, and logical arguments; seamless flow throughout.]{.mark}
