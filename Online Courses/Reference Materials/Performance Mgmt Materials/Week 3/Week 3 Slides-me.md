## Murphy, 2008 {#slide-19}

One-factor:  ratings = performance + random error

Solution: statistical correction

Problem: ignores systemic factors

Multi-factor:  multiple systematic factors influences ratings

Solution: identify and control non-performance factors

Focus: rater capability

Mediated Model (preferred):  raters intentionally distort ratings

Raters pursue goals other than accuracy

Solution: Change organizational incentives

Focus: Rater motivation

Problem isn\'t rater inability---it\'s rater unwillingness
