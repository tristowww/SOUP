## Realistic Job Previews {#slide-11}

-  Job applicants that represent the reality of the job and organization may have lower job application and acceptance rates, but those who take the job show higher job performance, job satisfaction, and lower turnover ( Breaugh , 2008).

-  Realistic Job Previews (RJPs) work best when:

<!-- -->

- Few applicants are hired 
- Entry level positions
- Unemployment is low
- 

<!-- -->

-  RJPs is most important with intrinsic factors like culture because it is more difficult to inflate extrinsic factors like pay

## Harrison et al. (2006) {#slide-19}

Meta-analysis reviewing research on employee attitudes towards affirmative action programs in employment

- Affirmative action plans  -- plans either voluntarily adopted or mandated by law that outline an organization's specific steps to ensure equal employment opportunities and promote equal representation within its workforce

Findings:

-  Overall support for affirmative action was positive but varied based on specific program features and individual characteristics 
-  Minority, female, and younger employees generally expressed stronger support
-  Concerns about reverse discrimination and program fairness negatively impacted attitudes
-  Programs with clear goals, transparent selection processes, and strong communication strategies received more positive evaluations
