## Selection as a Process {#slide-2}

2

- Ability: selection & training

- Selection is the process of  collecting and evaluating information about an  individual in order to extend an offer of employment. Such employment could be either  a first position  for a new employee or  a different  position for a current employee. The selection process is performed under  legal and market constraints  and address the  future interests  of the organization and of the individual.

## Selection System Process {#slide-7}

7

- Dynamic relationship between application and job/organization until employment 
- Two-way process where both parties actively communicate as decisions are made
- The organization is seeking to fill a position with the best candidate, while the applicant is seeking a job that fits their skills and career aspirations.
