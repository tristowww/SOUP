## Feldman & Lankau (2005) {#slide-25}

Executive coaching: A review and agenda for  future research

## What is  coaching ? {#slide-26}

"a process of equipping people with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities they need to develop themselves and become more effective" (Peterson & Hicks, 1995)

Initially method to address employee skill deficiency

Has transitioned to focusing on executive development

## 3 key elements of executive coaching {#slide-27}

One-on-one counseling focused on addressing issues related to work

Addresses strengths and weaknesses as a starting point for identifying development opportunities

Leverages identified development opportunities in order to drive efficiency and effectiveness
