## What is Coaching?  {#slide-6}

Coaching is NOT...

Mentoring 

Therapy 

Consulting

Coaching IS...

Professional coaching is a collaborative and transformative process designed to help individuals or teams achieve their full potential and reach their professional goals.

It helps people get "unstuck" through increasing self awareness, setting goals, and having accountability with a trusted partner.

## Acknowledging {#slide-14}

Benefits to Developing the Skill of Acknowledging 

Let's the other person know: 

You care enough about them to focus on  them  

You heard what they said 

You understand what they said

Acknowledging is a core skill of a great coach, trainer, teammate, and friend.

When people feel understood they will be more comfortable talking with you.

## Acknowledging {#slide-15}

Acknowledging is like paraphrasing or mirroring back what someone just said, but in a way that communicates that you heard the significance or deeper understanding behind what they said. 

Example

Client:  I have been trying to get my sales numbers up at work, I even stay late and watch videos on better approaches, but no matter what I do I can't seem to bring them up. 

Coach:  So, you are saying that you are putting in a big effort, but you aren't getting the results you want?

## Coach  & Trainer Skills {#slide-18}

::: {.smartart .vList2 layout="vList2"}
**Meet them where they are at: **

- Be able to adjust to where the trainees are 
- Assuming what someone else knows in training often leads to trainees learning less. 

**Dance in the conversation: **

- Start with a plan, but be able to adjust during the training based on what you are learning about the trainees.  
- Trainees learn more when the trainer is able to improvise and interact 
:::

## Coach & Trainer Skills {#slide-19}

::: {.smartart .vList2 layout="vList2"}
**Use Numerous Modalities**

- Using a variety of modalities often increases learning outcomes. 

**Focus on What's Important **

- You do not need to know everything, but you do need to know the critical components so you can navigate the trainee to keep focused 

**Ask Questions   **

- Encourages class engagement with the instructor and with each other. 
:::
