## Transfer of Training Theory {#slide-12}

Works best when there is high similarity between what is done in training and what is done on the job

- Closed skills-  Training objectives linked to specific skills that are identical to those on the job
- Open skills-  Training objectives linked to more general knowledge principles related to the job
- 
- Near transfer - Application of trained knowledge or skills to tasks very similar to those in training
- Far transfer - Application of trained knowledge or skills to tasks or contexts different from those in training
