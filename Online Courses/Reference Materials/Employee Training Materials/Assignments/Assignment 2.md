**[Key Concepts:]{.mark}**

- [**Near Transfer**: When the training context closely resembles the performance context, allowing trainees to apply skills in almost identical situations to those in which they were learned.]{.mark}

- [**Far Transfer**: When trainees must adapt and generalize principles from training to different situations or contexts than those in which they were originally learned.]{.mark}

- [**Closed Skills**: Skills with fixed procedures that should be performed the same way each time with minimal variation (e.g., following a specific safety protocol).]{.mark}

- [**Open Skills**: Skills that require adaptation to varying circumstances, where the correct application depends on the specific situation (e.g., customer service interactions).]{.mark}
