**[1 Mastercard --- "Unlocked" Internal Talent Marketplace]{.mark}**

**[A. Need for Upskilling]{.mark}**

- [**Driver 1 -- Digital-payments arms race.** Fin-tech challengers cut Mastercard's product‐cycle time from \~24 to 12 months, so the company needed deeper in-house software and data talent. → *Compressed skill half-life* (Dachner et al., 2021).]{.mark}

- [**Driver 2 -- Crisis redeployment.** COVID-19 froze travel-card revenue; redeploying staff internally avoided layoffs and preserved tacit knowledge. → *Volatility & resilience trend* (Week 15 slides).]{.mark}

**[B. AI & Technological Change (Jaiswal et al., 2022)]{.mark}**

- [**Cloud-native API engineering** -- *Technical Data-Manipulation* category. Powers open-banking products; rollout time ↓ 28 %.]{.mark}

- [**Human--AI compliance oversight** -- *Interfacing & Oversight* category. Teams audit credit-risk algorithms for bias, meeting EU AI-Act standards.]{.mark}

**[C. Best Practices & Outcomes]{.mark}**

1.  [**Learning-in-the-Flow.** Gloat gigs, nano-modules, and mentors surface directly in Workday. → Internal transfers ↑ 47 %.]{.mark}

2.  [**Culture of Lifelong Learning.** "Skills Wallet" funds MOOCs; quarterly town-halls celebrate badge earners. → Engagement item *"I can grow here"* ↑ 15 pts year-over-year.]{.mark}

**[B. AI-Era Skills]{.mark}**
