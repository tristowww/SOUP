**Objective:**

[This assignment focuses on training design and implementation by having you develop a Training Design Roadmap/Outline for a training topic of your choice, ensuring it incorporates best practices in learner engagement, transfer of training, and knowledge retention.]{.mark}

[As always, you are allowed to work collaboratively in groups for this assignment but must turn in your own documents and not simply copy all responses.]{.mark}

**Instructions:**

1.  **[Select a Training Topic]{.mark}**

[Choose a topic you feel interested in and generally knowledgeable about. This can be relevant to I/O psychology (DEI, Leadership, Culture, etc.), it can be generally relevant to the workplace (Customer service training for servers, Internet safety, Conflict resolution skills, etc.), or it can just be a topic you are interested in (Mario Kart, Volleyball, Miniatures Painting, etc.).]{.mark}

- [What topic did you choose?]{.mark}

- [What is the specific training outcome/purpose and the intended audience?]{.mark}

2.  **[Develop a Training Outline/Roadmap]{.mark}**

[Create a training roadmap or outline for your chosen topic, it can be more graphicly oriented, or it can be an outline. Refer to what concepts are included in some of the slide examples. Make sure you at least include:]{.mark}

- [A major learning objective for what trainers should know or be able to do after training.]{.mark}

- [At least 3 intermediate learning goals with details on how that will be achieved through training]{.mark}

- [Specify the length of time the training will span, as well as any important milestones]{.mark}

- [Include any KSAOs or materials trainees should be prepared with before training and what materials the training will provide]{.mark}

3.  **[Training Design Considerations]{.mark}**

[Consider how the training can be structured to best promote learning and transfer of training outcomes.]{.mark}

- [What environment will the training take place in (on-site or off-site) and how will this format match with the type of training?]{.mark}

- [What are one or two specific activities planned for the training?]{.mark}

4.  **[Training Engagement]{.mark}**

[Consider the elements that can make the training more engaging for the trainees.]{.mark}

- [Which training structure will be best for your training program (learner-content interaction, learner-learner interaction, or learner-instructor interaction)?]{.mark}

- [How will you incorporate elements of 'fun' into the training?]{.mark}

- [What are some trainee considerations at the individual level specific to your chosen training?]{.mark}

5.  **[Training Practice and Knowledge Retention]{.mark}**

[Identify how to best keep trainees practicing and retaining the outcomes of your training.]{.mark}

[Use **Donovan & Radosevich (1999)** findings on **distributed vs. massed practice**:]{.mark}

- [Will you use spaced learning? If so, how?]{.mark}

- [How will you help trainees retain the training content (e.g., microlearning, mnemonic devices, post-training assignments, resources)?]{.mark}
