## Grandey , 2000 {#slide-22}

- Emotional regulation theory applied to emotional labor

<!-- -->

- Broader form of emotional labor that can be used not just to meet job demands, but also to achieve personal goals
- Process of individuals influencing which emotions they have, when they have them, and how they experience and express them
- Deep-level strategies and Surface-level strategies
-

## Grandey, 2000 {#slide-23}

  Characteristic   Emotional labor                                  Emotional regulation
  ---------------- ------------------------------------------------ ---------------------------------------------------
  Definition       Managing emotions to meet the demands of a job   Managing emotions in a healthy and productive way
  Context          Workplace                                        Any context
  Motivation       External (i.e., job requirements)                Internal (i.e., personal goals)
  Goals            To meet the expectations of others               To improve one\'s own well-being and productivity
