# Course 1: Structured Hiring That Works

## Full Title
Structured Hiring That Works: Build an Evidence-Based Selection System

## Launch Priority
Launch first (Month 3-4)

## Price Tier
Standard ($197-297)

---

## Audience

**Primary:** HR Professionals (Segment 1), People Managers (Segment 2)
**Secondary:** Small Business Owners (Segment 3), Consultants (Segment 8), Tech People Ops (Segment 7)

---

## Differentiator

Most hiring courses teach "tips and tricks." This course teaches the science of why structured methods outperform intuition, backed by 85+ years of validity research (Schmidt & Hunter), and provides the actual frameworks (Campion's 15 components) to build a defensible, effective system. Teresa's NLP research on job ad deception adds unique credibility.

---

## Core Topic Clusters

- 1.2: Selection Methods & Measures (structured interviews, biodata, cognitive ability tests, work simulations)
- 1.3: Measurement & Decision Making (reliability, validity, mechanical vs. clinical judgment, score banding)
- 1.4: Legal Compliance & Fairness (adverse impact, 4/5ths rule, validity-diversity tradeoff, bias mitigation)

---
