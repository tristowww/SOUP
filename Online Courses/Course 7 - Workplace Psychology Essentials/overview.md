# Course 7: Workplace Psychology Essentials

## Full Title
Workplace Psychology Essentials: The Science of Managing People

## Launch Priority
Gateway course (Month 2-3) - launches before all others to build email list

## Price Tier
Micro ($49-97); or free/low-cost as lead magnet for Courses 1-3

---

## Audience

**Primary:** Small Business Owners (Segment 3), People Managers (Segment 2)
**Secondary:** Nonprofit Leaders (Segment 5)

---

## Differentiator

An accessible, jargon-free introduction to organizational psychology for non-psychologists. Not another "management tips" course; this teaches the actual science behind why people behave the way they do at work, and what managers can do about it. Each module ends with a concrete action step.

---

## Core Topic Clusters

- 4.1: Work Attitudes & Engagement (job satisfaction, organizational justice, OCB/CWB, commitment)
- 4.2: Organizational Culture & Climate (culture vs. climate, culture models)
- 4.3: Employee Wellbeing (work stress, emotional labor, work-life interface, recovery)
- 2.4: Motivation & Development (expectancy theory, equity theory, goal-setting)

---

## Format

Self-paced micro-course series, 4-5 modules of 30-45 minutes each. Designed for busy operators who learn in short bursts.

---

## Adaptation Notes

This course requires the heaviest adaptation of any course:
- Strip ALL academic framing
- Remove all psychometric terminology
- Replace with step-by-step guides
- Add scenario-based examples from small/mid-size company contexts
- Create "quick start" checklists
- Shorten modules to 30-45 minutes max
- Focus on "templates you can use today"
