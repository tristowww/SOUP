# Course 3: Training That Transfers

## Full Title
Training That Transfers: Design, Deliver, and Measure Learning Impact

## Launch Priority
Phase 2 (Month 5-6)

## Price Tier
Standard ($197-297)

---

## Audience

**Primary:** L&D Professionals (Segment 9)
**Secondary:** HR Professionals (Segment 1), Consultants (Segment 8), Nonprofit Leaders (Segment 5)

---

## Differentiator

Most training-the-trainer courses focus on delivery techniques. This course starts with needs assessment (the step 94% of organizations skip), moves through evidence-based design principles (transfer theory, adult learning), and ends with multilevel evaluation beyond Kirkpatrick Level 1. Teresa's research on training effectiveness meta-analyses provides unique depth.

---

## Core Topic Clusters

- 3.1: Training Foundations (training vs. development, 70-20-10 model, effectiveness framework)
- 3.2: Training Needs & Design (3-component needs assessment, design principles, transfer of training)
- 3.3: Delivery Methods (traditional, group, technology-based, emerging methods)
- 3.5: Training Evaluation (Kirkpatrick 4 levels, ROI, multilevel evaluation)

---

## Format

Self-paced, 6-8 modules, ~10 hours total content. Each module includes a "Build Your Training" progressive project.
