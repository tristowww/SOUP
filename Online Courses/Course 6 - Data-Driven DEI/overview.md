## Price Tier

---

## Audience

**Primary:** DEI Practitioners (Segment 6)
**Secondary:** HR Professionals (Segment 1), Consultants (Segment 8), Nonprofit Leaders (Segment 5)

---

## Differentiator

Most DEI courses are awareness-based ("unconscious bias training"). This course starts with measurement (adverse impact analysis, inclusion climate surveys), moves through evidence-based interventions (backfire prevention, perspective-taking research), and introduces AI-enabled inclusion (Teresa's AIRA framework from her forthcoming OUP chapter). The data-driven approach appeals to organizations demanding ROI from DEI spending.

---

## Core Topic Clusters

- 5.1: DEI Training & Interventions (unconscious bias training, perspective-taking, backfire prevention)
- 5.2: DEI Analytics & Measurement (adverse impact analysis, inclusion climate measurement, disability inclusion)
- 5.3: AI-Enabled Inclusion (AIRA Framework, AI for accessibility, algorithmic bias detection)
- 1.4: Legal Compliance & Fairness (adverse impact, 4/5ths rule)
- 2.5: Bias, Fairness, & Ethics (17+ bias types, justice framework, algorithmic bias)

---

## Format

Cohort-based, 6-week program, weekly 90-min live sessions + self-paced modules between sessions. Cohort model supports accountability and peer learning on sensitive topics.
